Noto is a website that suggests music based on whatever you type in. Just enter a prompt—whether it's a mood, a place, or even a story—and Noto curates tracks that capture the vibe. It's a smarter, more creative way to discover songs, turning your words into soundscapes that feel personal and fresh.

Noto was created in response to a widespread frustration: the inability of many platforms to accurately interpret and deliver on user prompts. Traditional music platform search engines often process queries too literally, failing to capture the user's intent. Noto addresses this gap by providing a more intuitive, context-aware approach to music discovery.

We use Spotify, Last.fm, and Gemini's artificial intelligence to suggest and curate songs especially for you.
